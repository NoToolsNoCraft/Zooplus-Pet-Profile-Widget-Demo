Unlock the Power of Personalization

A simple, elegant solution to transform how customers engage with the Zooplus Pet Profile feature.

The Challenge: A Hidden Gem

The Zooplus Pet Profile is a powerful tool for customer engagement and loyalty, but it's currently a hidden feature. Tucked away in the account settings, it's missed by new visitors and non-logged-in shoppers.

Low Visibility

Many users never discover the feature, leading to lost opportunities for personalization.

High Friction

The process of finding and filling out the profile requires multiple steps and logins.

Missed Value

Customers aren't immediately shown the benefits of creating a pet profile.

The Solution: A Seamless Integration

The Slide-in Panel

A clean, responsive panel that slides in from the side, guiding the user through a quick and easy pet profile quiz. It's a frictionless, engaging experience that captures essential data and immediately presents personalized results.

The Sticky Button

A persistent, floating button that remains in the corner of the screen. Its bold design and clear call-to-action ("Create my pet profile") act as a constant, non-intrusive reminder for both new and returning visitors.

How It Works: A Dynamic Experience

The slide-in panel intelligently adapts to the user's state, providing a tailored and highly relevant experience.

For Logged-Out Users

When a user is not logged in, the widget presents a quick, multi-step pet profile creation process.

  • • Step 1: The user quickly answers a few questions about their pet.
  • • Step 2: The panel immediately displays personalized product recommendations.
  • • Step 3: A clear call-to-action encourages the user to log in or create an account to save their new pet profile.

For Logged-In Users

For logged-in users, the widget provides a shortcut to their saved information, enhancing the shopping experience.

  • • Case 1: The user has existing pet profiles. The panel displays their saved pets.
  • • Case 2: The user has no saved profiles. The panel prompts them to create one, just like the logged-out experience.
  • • This streamlines personalization and reinforces the value of the profile feature.
